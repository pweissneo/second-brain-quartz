# The Ownership Mindset

Taking full responsibility for your knowledge.

## The Mindset Shift

Before: Knowledge happens to you
- Take courses someone else designed
- Read books someone else recommended
- Learn what "they" say is important

After: You own your learning
- Design your own curriculum
- Connect what matters to you
- Build your own knowledge

## Ownership Actions

### 1. Curate Your Inputs
- Choose what to consume
- Don't just follow trends

### 2. Process Actively
- Don't just save - understand
- Write in your own words

### 3. Build Your Structure
- Create your own categories
- Link what makes sense to you
- Don't copy others' structures

### 4. Share Your Perspective
- Add your interpretation
- Don't just regurgitate
- Build on others' ideas

## Why It Matters

When you own your knowledge:
- You remember more
- You think better
- You create more
- You stand out
