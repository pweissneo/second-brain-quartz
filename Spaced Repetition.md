# Spaced Repetition

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that schedules review at increasing intervals to optimize long-term memory retention.

## The Science

Based on the **forgetting curve** (Ebbinghaus, 1885): information decays exponentially without reinforcement. Spaced repetition counters this by reviewing material just before forgetting.

## Intervals

| Review | Interval |
|--------|----------|
| 1st | 1 day |
| 2nd | 3 days |
| 3rd | 7 days |
| 4th | 14 days |
| 5th | 30 days |
