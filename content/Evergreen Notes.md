# Evergreen Notes

Evergreen notes are notes that are continuously improved and expanded over time, unlike static notes that become outdated.

## Concept (Andy Matuschak)

Key principles:
- Write as if for your future self
- Notes should be self-contained and understandable alone
- Iterate on notes rather than abandoning them
- Link forward to developing ideas

## Lifecycle

```
Seedling → Bud → Evergreen
   ↓         ↓         ↓
  Raw     Developing  Mature
 idea     concept    knowledge
```

## In Practice

- Start with a seed note (small, focused)
- Expand when new connections emerge
- Merge similar notes to reduce fragmentation
- Update outdated information on review
