# Compound Knowledge Effects

How small knowledge gains compound over time.

## The Compound Equation

Knowledge builds on knowledge:

```
Day 1: 1 note → 1 connection
Day 30: 30 notes → 90 connections (compound!)
Day 100: 100 notes → 500+ connections (emergence!)
```

## Why It Compounds

### 1. Network Effects
Each new note connects to existing notes:
- 1 note: 0-2 connections
- 10 notes: ~15 connections
- 50 notes: ~200 connections

### 2. Synthesis
Combining existing notes creates new insights:
- Note A + Note B → Insight C
- This is [[Emergence in Knowledge Graphs]]

### 3. Faster Processing
More context = faster note creation:
- First note: 15 minutes
- 50th note: 5 minutes
- 100th note: 2 minutes

## The Tipping Point

Around 50-100 notes, something shifts:
- Notes start connecting organically
- Insights emerge without forcing
- System becomes self-sustaining

## Implications

1. **Start small** - 1 note/day compounds
2. **Be patient** - Effects take time
3. **Don't skip days** - Consistency matters
4. **Trust the process** - Compound effects are real

This is similar to a [[The Knowledge Flywheel|flywheel effect]] — once momentum builds, the system becomes self-sustaining.
