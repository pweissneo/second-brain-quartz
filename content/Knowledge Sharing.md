# Knowledge Sharing

Sharing your knowledge base insights with others effectively.

## Why Share

Benefits of sharing:
- Forces deeper understanding
- Builds reputation
- Helps others learn
- Creates feedback loops

---

## Sharing Methods

### 1. Direct Teaching
- Explain concepts to colleagues
- Pair programming with knowledge links
- Mentorship using your notes

### 2. Content Creation
- Blog posts from atomic notes
- Thread summaries on social media
- Video explanations

### 3. Collaborative Editing
- Invite others to contribute
- Enable feedback through comments

---

## What to Share

| Share This | Don't Share This |
|------------|-----------------|
| Atomic concepts | Raw inbox items |
| Synthesized insights | Unprocessed captures |
| Proven patterns | First drafts |
| Templates | Personal context |

---

## Quality Check Before Sharing

1. **Atomic?** - One idea per piece
2. **Linked?** - Connections clear
3. **Sourced?** - Claims backed
4. **Readable?** - Self-contained
