# The Networked Mind

Your second brain extends your mind into the network.

## The Extended Mind

Just as telescopes extend vision and computers extend computation:
- Notes extend memory
- Links extend association
- Search extends recall

## Network Effects

Your mind + networked tools = networked mind:
- Access beyond biological limits
- Recall beyond biological capacity
- Intelligence beyond individual brain

## The Shift

From:
- Brain stores everything
- Limited by skull

To:
- Brain + second brain
- Limited by network

## Implications

1. **Externalize** - Don't memorize, organize
2. **Network** - Connect ideas, don't just collect
3. **Augment** - Use tools to think better

## The Future

Soon, AI assistants will:
- Query your knowledge
- Find connections you miss
- Surface insights automatically

Your second brain is the foundation.
