# AI-Assisted Connection Suggestion

AI can suggest meaningful connections between notes that humans might miss.

## Capability

AI systems can analyze notes and suggest:
- Related concepts based on semantic similarity
- Contrast points with existing notes
- Analogy bridges between domains
- Category groupings for MOCs

## Quality Criteria

**Test:** Can AI suggest connections humans would value?
- Check: Are suggested connections surprising but valid?
- Check: Can you explain why the connection makes sense?
- Check: Do suggestions increase graph density without inflation?

## How It Works

1. **Semantic analysis** — AI reads notes, identifies key concepts
2. **Similarity scoring** — AI compares concepts across notes
3. **Suggestion generation** — AI proposes connections above threshold
4. **Human validation** — Human confirms or rejects suggestions

## Best Practices

- AI suggests, human confirms — don't auto-link
- Prioritize cross-domain connections (highest value)
- Review suggestions regularly (weekly)
