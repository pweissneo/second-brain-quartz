# The Modular Mind

Your second brain as external memory.

## The Brain Can't Hold Everything

Cognitive limits:
- Working memory: 7±2 items
- Long-term: Finite but large
- Retrieval: Imperfect

## External Memory Benefits

### 1. Offload
Store what you don't need to remember:
- Facts, formulas, references
- Free brain for thinking
- "Google for your mind"

### 2. Extend
Go beyond biological limits:
- Access more information
- Make connections you'd miss
- Process more complexity

### 3. Accelerate
Faster access and retrieval:
- Instant search
- Structured linking
- AI-assisted recall

## The Hybrid Mind

Combine biological and external:
- Brain: Creativity, judgment, insight
- Second brain: Storage, retrieval, structure

## Best Practice

Use both effectively:
- Don't over-rely on external (atrophies recall)
- Don't under-rely (wastes cognitive resources)
- Let brain do what it does best
