# Daily Review Practice

Making review a habit for better knowledge retention.

## Why Daily Review

Daily review reinforces [[The Forgetting Curve]]:
- Spaced repetition through regular engagement
- Updates outdated information
- Creates new connections

## The 10-Minute Rule

10 minutes daily > 1 hour weekly:

| Frequency | Effectiveness |
|----------|--------------|
| Daily 10 min | High retention |
| Weekly 1 hour | Medium retention |
| Monthly | Low retention |

## Review Protocol

### Morning (5 min)
1. Check inbox - process new captures
2. Review 3 random notes
3. Add 1 new link if possible

### Evening (5 min)
1. Add new notes from today
2. Check for gaps in recent notes
3. Update any stale notes

## What to Review

- Recent notes (last 7 days)
- High-value hubs
- Isolated notes
- Notes with broken links
