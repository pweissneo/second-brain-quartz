# The Knowledge Legacy

> **DEPRECATED:** This note has been deprecated. The useful content about documenting, sharing, and updating knowledge has been integrated into [[Knowledge Sharing]].

Building something that outlasts you.

## Why Legacy Matters

Your knowledge can outlive you:
- Help others learn from your experience
- Your insights continue after you're gone
- Build on others' work, enable others to build on yours

## The Chain of Knowledge

```
Ancient scholars → Middle Ages → Enlightenment → Modern → You
```

Each generation builds on the last.

Your second brain continues this:
- Your notes become available to others
- Your insights help future learners

## Building the Legacy

1. **Document clearly** - Write for others
2. **Open share** - Make notes accessible
3. **Connect broadly** - Link across domains
4. **Update regularly** - Keep notes alive

## The Buddhist Library

The Tibetan Buddhist tradition:
- Thousands of years old
- Still copied by hand
- Preserved through generations

Your digital notes can achieve similar longevity.

## Your Children

Not just biological:
- Your intellectual children
- Your students
- Your readers
- Anyone who benefits from your notes
